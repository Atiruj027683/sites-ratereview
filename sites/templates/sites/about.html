Rate & Review

Rate & Review is an university project in the Software Engineering Principal of King Mongkut's Institute of Technology Ladkrabang. The website is capable of rate and review topics in the category of "Recipes", "Movies" and "Book". Users can create their own account and start created their own topic. Also rating and reviewing others topic is also allowed. In the further future, If the project succeed, it can be more further expands.
